Short Trip to Front-End

Always dreamed of learning how to design and code your own website, but too busy to take classes? Embark on July 17th for Le Wagon Summer Edition - Short Trip to Front-End!

Journey description

A 2-weeks intensive bootcamp, every day (8:30am - 6:30pm) from July 17th until July 28th. All sessions are entirely taught in English.

Scheduled stops

Git, GitHub, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, UX/UI, HTTP, AJAX, Deployment, URL customization, etc.

Final destination

A website designed, coded and deployed, from A to Z, by your own hands. You will showcase your creations on the last day !
